About

During this Covid-19 pandemic its being repeatedly advised to stay indoors as much as possible and only go out only when totally necessary to keep everyone safe. Our product is promoting this norm to keep everyone indoors. For example, if a person is showing symptoms of malaria in a normal situation, he/she will need to go to a nearby hospital and give the required tests but presently going to hospitals should be avoided as much as possible because the chances of catching Coronavirus there is higher if compared to a normal place. In other case, the patient can register online and use our product and get their result within minutes whether they have Malaria or not. Our product has a high accuracy rate and is very easy to use even for a compounder or anyone with no or less medical knowledge can run our product easily and comfortably. Therefore, reducing the workload of doctors in these stressful times.
